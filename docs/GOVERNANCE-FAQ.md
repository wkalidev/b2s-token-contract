## Proposals

### Who can create proposals?

Anyone with **10,000+ staked $B2S tokens** can create proposals.

### How long is the voting period?

**7 days** (~1,008 blocks on Stacks blockchain)

### What happens if my proposal fails?

Nothing negative! Failed proposals simply don't get implemented. You can refine and resubmit.

### Can I edit my proposal after submitting?

No, proposals are immutable once submitted. Double-check before creating!

## Voting

### How do I vote?

1. Connect wallet
2. Browse active proposals
3. Click "Vote Yes" or "Vote No"
4. Confirm transaction

### Can I change my vote?

No, votes are final and immutable.

### Can I vote multiple times?

No, one vote per proposal per address.

### Do I need to pay gas fees?

Yes, small transaction fee (~0.001 STX) to record your vote on-chain.

### What if I don't have staked tokens?

You need to stake $B2S tokens first to gain voting power.

## Results

### What is quorum?

**20% of total staked tokens** must participate in voting for the proposal to be valid.

### What is the approval threshold?

**51% or more YES votes** are required for a proposal to pass.

### When are results final?

After the 7-day voting period ends and votes are counted.

### Who executes passed proposals?

Core team executes on-chain changes. Off-chain changes require coordination.

## Technical

### Is voting on-chain?

Yes! All votes are recorded on the Stacks blockchain.

### Can I verify votes?

Yes, all votes are public and verifiable on the blockchain explorer.

### Is my vote anonymous?

No, votes are linked to your wallet address and publicly visible.

### What prevents vote manipulation?

- One vote per address
- Voting power based on stake
- On-chain verification
- Transparent counting

## Troubleshooting

### "Insufficient stake" error

You need at least 10,000 staked $B2S to create proposals.

### "Already voted" error

You've already voted on this proposal. Votes are final.

### "Voting closed" error

The 7-day voting period has ended.

### Transaction fails

- Check wallet connection
- Ensure sufficient STX for gas
- Verify on testnet network
